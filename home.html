About Runner

We are an athletics manufacturing company named Runner that produces and sells athletic wear. We were established in 1992. The footwear we produce are engineered to maximize comfort and grip. The clothing we create are designed to help provide breathability, durability, protection, comfort, and a wide range of motion. Our corporate headquarters is located in Los Angeles, but we have store fronts in San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego. We only market clothing and footwear in the United States. Our goal is to provide the best quality and fashion of athletic footwear, so that the general public can be both comfortable and stylish during any physical exercise.
