Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best quality and fashion of athletic footwear, so that the general public can be both comfortable and stylish during any physical exercise.

About Runner

We are an athletics manufacturing company named Runner that produces and sells athletic wear. We were established in 1992. he footwear we produce are engineered to maximize comfort and grip. The clothing we create are designed to help provide breathability, durability, protection, comfort, and a wide range of motion. Our corporate headquarters is located in Los Angeles, but we have store fronts in San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego. We only market clothing and footwear in the United States. We have been manufacturing and selling athletic wear since 1992 til present day (2020).

Community Impact

We have impacted our community greatly. For every purchase in our company 10 cents is donated to cancer research to help fight this disease. In addition, our company trys to recruit employees with any type of background. Those with family struggles are provided with neccesities to survive.
